About Company

While the concept of balanced and nutritious cattle feed was relatively unknown to the Indian farmers, we recognized the need for a reliable and high-quality feed to enhance livestock health and productivity. Our journey began with the goal of educating farmers about the significant benefits of well-balanced cattle feed and providing them with affordable, effective solutions.

Though we are new to the market, Krushiganda Agro has already gained recognition for its commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
